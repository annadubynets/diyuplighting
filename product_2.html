Rent Marquee Lights

These jumbo marquee letters measure 21" tall and are made from thin sturdy sheet metal. The 5-watt Edison style filament glass bulbs give them a warm and inviting amber glow. Each character rents for just $49 including free shipping both ways. Our marquee numbers and letters are perfect for any occasion.
